What's new

Distance picker + filters.

One sheet for everything
Tap Exact ▾ in the search bar to open a single bottom sheet. Distance lives on top (the primary affordance, since it's what the pill claims), and the rest of the filter controls sit below. No separate filter screen, no chrome creep on the home page.
Distance as a proper picker
Six options as a radio list: Exact, 0.5, 1, 2, 5, 10 miles. Each row has a one-line hint ("Same street & immediate neighbours" etc.) so the choice means something — instead of a row of bare numbers.
Filters that earn their place
Property type (multi-select chips), bedrooms (min), EPC band (min, with the official band colours), HealthScore minimum (slider, snaps in fives), and a "Verified Passport only" toggle. No price, no listing-status pills, no clutter.
Draft state — Apply commits
All changes inside the sheet are a draft. Apply commits them (updates the pill label, shows the count badge, and reveals a summary chip row above results). Cancel or backdrop tap discards. Reset clears the draft back to defaults.
Feedback on the pill
When filters are active the "Exact ▾" pill turns teal, shows the new distance label (e.g. "1 mi") and a small gold dot to signal that filters are layered on top of distance. A summary chip row appears under the search so the user can remove individual filters without re-opening the sheet.
Try it
Tap Exact ▾. Pick a distance. Toggle a few filter chips. Drag the HealthScore slider. Tap Apply. Then look at the pill label and the summary row. Tap an "x" on a summary chip to remove just that filter. Tap the pill again — your selections are remembered.
