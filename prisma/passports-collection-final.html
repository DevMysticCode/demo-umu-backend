What's new in v6

  • First-visit onboarding tour. When a user opens this screen for the first time ever, the lady walks them through 4 stops: the resume card ("Continue your work"), the filter row ("Filter by status"), the compliant badge on tile-2 ("This badge means everything's complete"), and finally the hero stats ("Tap and hold anything for a tip like this — try this row"). Each stop spotlights its target with a gold ring and dims the rest of the screen, while a card with the lady appears next to it with a title, body, step counter, Skip and Next buttons.
  • Smooth spotlight transitions. The spotlight rectangle moves from one target to the next with a 0.55s eased animation. The tip card slides into its new position on the same curve. No jumpy disappear/reappear — the experience feels like the lady walking from element to element.
  • Step indicator with dot pagination. "1 of 4" with four small dots, the current dot filled in. Standard pattern users will recognise from iOS guided tours.
  • localStorage for "first time" tracking. A flag (umu_passport_tour_completed_v1) is set when the tour completes or is skipped. Auto-run only happens when the flag is absent. The "Replay onboarding tour" button outside the phone clears the flag and re-runs.
  • Permanent "?" button in the nav bar. A small help icon between the search button and the avatar. Tap it to replay the tour at any time. This is the production-ready way back to the tour for users who skipped it or forgot the gestures.
  • Tour suppresses the page-load celebration on first visit. The tour IS the first impression on first visit; the celebration on tile-2 plays for returning users as the welcome moment. Two different welcomes for two different users.
  • Skip and Got it button. Skip ends the tour immediately at any point. The final step's primary button changes to "Got it ✓" instead of "Next →" to clearly mark the end. Both record the same flag in storage.
  • Esc key dismisses the tour. Standard keyboard accessibility.
