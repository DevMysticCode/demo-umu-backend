⚖️
Tenanted sales must disclose tenancy to buyers under Consumer Protection from Unfair Trading Regulations 2008. The AST transfers to the new owner unless vacant possession is obtained via Section 21 (no fault) or Section 8 (with grounds). The Renters' Rights Act 2024 phases out Section 21 — check current possession routes before listing.
