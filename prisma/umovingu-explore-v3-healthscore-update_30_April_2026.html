What is a Property Passport?
📘
Think of it like a car's logbook — but for your home.
A permanent record that stays with the property, not the transaction. Every certificate, every improvement, every check — all in one place. You own the data. You control who sees it.
Without a Passport, here's what happens:
💸
You lose thousands
Problems discovered late — after surveys, legal fees and months of stress. Average fall-through costs £2,700. And that's before the deal collapses.
😰
You have no control
Solicitors, surveyors and buyers decide the pace — and the narrative. You're reacting, not leading.
🕳️
Hidden surprises sink deals
1 in 3 sales collapse. Usually because something was found too late — something that could have been sorted months earlier, cheaply.
With a Passport, you're in control:
🗓️
Prepare years ahead — not weeks before
Start as an owner occupier. Build your record gradually. When you're ready to sell, it's already done.
🔒
You own the data — you choose who sees it
Share with your solicitor, your agent, or a specific buyer. One tap. Revoke access any time.
💷
Save thousands — not spend them
Issues dealt with upfront cost a fraction of what they cost when discovered during a sale. Know now. Fix now. Sell with confidence.
⚡
Close in 14 days, not 179
By the time you accept an offer, everything is already verified. Solicitors just review — nothing left to dig up.
It stays with the home — forever.
When you sell, your Passport transfers to the next owner. The home's story continues. Its value is preserved. Future buyers trust it before they even view it.
