v2.3 · balayage + lift

What changed.

Same chrome as Explore
Header pattern (back button + eyebrow pill), section-headers, card radius, border colours, gradient tints — all match Explore v2.4 exactly. The two screens now read as siblings of one product, not separate designs.
Score card replaces six competing zones
The original result page had a navy stat slab, teal overpay block, red gauge, yellow EPC alert, grey renovation card, green/black interest picker, yellow claim banner, and purple Passport block all in the same scroll. v1 collapses them into one calm score card (gauge + band + explainer + data-quality note + two action buttons) so the user knows what they're looking at and what to do next.
Score colour ladder, not a rainbow
The score card tints itself based on the band: red below 40, amber 40–70, teal above 70. The breakdown bars use the same three colours instead of one colour per metric. Less visual noise, more meaning.
Owner / buyer fork demoted from hero to button row
Was two big cards taking 40% of the page. Now it's two equal-weight buttons at the foot of the score card: "Get my real score" (filled teal) for owners, "Watch this" (outlined) for buyers. Same decision, less screen real estate, no choice fatigue.
Overpay block leads (back to its original job)
The big teal "You could be overpaying £1,827 / year" hero is the page's hook — what makes the user lean in. v1.1 restores it as the lead, full-bleed teal gradient, large white number, white CTA button on top. The score gauge then sits below as supporting evidence — calmly. One big moment, then the calm cohesive language for everything else.
EPC quality warning folds in
The yellow "Public EPC · improvements not yet reflected" alert is now a small grey sub-line inside the score card with a clear "Refine in 2 min →" link. Same information, calmer presentation.
Passport promote card is a sibling
The "No verified Passport yet" + "What's in a Passport" double-card is merged into a single navy-CTA card carrying the same mini-Passport icon and "Sells 12 weeks faster" copy as the Explore feature card. Same brand voice, same visual.
Two live signals, two scopes
A centred pulse-line is now under the trust block on the search screen: "147 HomeScores run in the last hour". Same component as the Explore live counter — ties the two pages together and gives the funnel entry a sense of momentum. On the result detail page, the per-property pulse stays in the address pill row ("4 checked today") so each surface gets the most useful scope.
Try it
Toggle 1 → 5 above. Tap a result on screen 2 to jump to the score detail. Tap the white CTA on the teal hero on screen 3 to land on running costs (4). Tap "Watch this" on either screen 3 or 4 to land on the buyer-watch screen (5). Toggle "For buyers / For owners" on screen 1 to swap the how-it-works steps.
